Social Media Plays Cupid As Moj Stars Divya And Pankaj Get Engaged

The grand engagement ceremony was held on 23rd April at the luxurious Royal Resort, Patalpani, Mhow, where the sweethearts exchanged rings.The couple made their grand entries in style, emerging from the centre of a giant red heart, while the entire venue lit up with sparklers and firework, making it a dreamy evening to remember. Divya was dressed in a designer gown, embroidered in gorgeous stonework, while PJ dazzled the big day in a smart red suit with a striking stone embroidery detail to match his lovely fiancee. From her jewellery, headband and even nails to his watch, stonework was the focus of the day, as the happy couple sparkled their way into the new phase of life. The event was graced by some of the biggest social media celebrities, such as Oye Indori, Poorvi Bhargav, Shikha Sharma, Ansh Pandit, Ashwin Yadav, Shivani Kapila, Sajid Ali, Sonal Agarwal, Chiku Vyas to name a few.
